Diwali Celebrations

सभी के दीप सुंदर है, हमारे क्या तुम्हारे क्या । उजाला हर तरफ है,इस किनारे क्या उस किनारे क्या ।।

Diwali is the festival of lights which symbolizes the victory of inner light over the spiritual darkness. It is the time when families and friends unite together and spread the message of brotherhood, love and unity. To adhere to the same, team "Anokhi पहल " decided to do something for the society. Not everyone is fortunate enough to experience Diwali the way we do. A small step from our side can make their lives a lot happier. Let's pledge to bring smiles on as many faces as we can and give our best to the society.
