उत्थान

शिक्षा सिर्फ किसी व्यक्ति विशेष का ही नहीं अपितु समस्त समाज के उत्थान का कारण बनती है। अनोखी पहल' द्वारा शुरू की गई एक महिम है जिसके तहत जरूरतमंद और मेधावी छात्रों को निःशुल्क शिक्षा प्रदान की जाएगी।

उत्थान से जुड़ी जरूरी बातें

1) इसका हिस्सा बनने हेतु राजकीय विद्यालयों में कक्षा 8, 9, 10 व 11 में पढ़ रहे मेधावी छात्रों का चुनाव एक परीक्षा के माध्यम से होगा।

2) चुने गए छात्रों को अनोखी पहल के शिक्षकों द्वारा निशुल्क शिक्षा प्रदान की जाएगी।

3) चयनित छात्रों की पढ़ाई के अतिरिक्त उनकी पाठ्येतर गतिविधियों

पर भी ध्यान दिया जाएगा।

Antyodaya

Conducted on the eve of Children’s Day, Antyodaya provides a platform to test the skills of students ranging from academic excellence to creative dexterity and physical fitness to mental fortitude.

We were delighted to have witnessed the participation of more than 500 enthusiastic students from over 16 schools. Some of the major participants include: GGIC Katra, Narayani Ashram Balika Inter College, Bal Bharti School, GGIC Civil Lines and BBS. We also witnessed students participating from various NGOs who work to educate poor children, and were delighted to receive such enthusiastic participation from Robin Hood Army(RHA) and Aarohan Foundation.

We are grateful to all the students for their exuberant participation and outstanding performance. Besides all these, the children also enjoyed various fun activities. We would like to thank all our team members who have been the pillars of support for all our activities.

Donations

Team Anokhi pehel conducted a clothes donation drive across various places in Allahabad. Cheerfully volunteered by students across all years in the task of collection, sorting, and distribution of clothes, we feel very proud that people really responded to our appeal of bringing clothes for donation.

The donation was immensely valuable and it was surprisingly suited to all age groups. Today was a special day not only because we donated clothes but also because we got the chance of interacting with some of the very marginalized communities of our society.

From families of snake charmers to old-aged patients of leprosy and Alzheimer's. We felt the need for a more inclusive and vast education program as a surprisingly high number of kids were not even enrolled in any educational background. It enhanced our commitment to keep working in this direction.

Jal Shakti Abhiyaan

Anokhi Pehel's main motive as an organization is to contribute as much to the society as we can. Under the "Jal Shakti Abhiyaan" children were taught about the scarcity of fresh water and how to save water in their day to day lives.

Also, they were advised to make less use of plastic and how these things affect the environment. We also had a chit-chat with the locals and their problems so that we can help them in every way possible.

Other than this, we also told parents about the importance of education and encouraged them to impart proper education to their children.

Children’s Day Celebration

This Children’s Day, here at Anokhi Pehel, has been a little bit more special. This year we organized all of our events online, fingers crossed in eager anticipation.

To our pleasant surprise, around 200 students participated in our Children’s Day Celebrations on 11-12 November, 2020.We are also glad to have received participants not just from Prayagraj, but from various places across India including Madhya Pradesh and Kerala. Online Classes in Lockdown Period

Started online classes since the past two days and have been giving our best to help our students. Many of them have already started learning and engaging with us.

However, there are many more students who are in need of proper structure and guidance during these tough times. We are always motivated to help out more and more students, especially who do not often have the necessary facilities. Our structure of online classes is made so that everyone is able to attend the classes with a simple phone with considerably lower internet speeds. We have also been helping out students on a simple call. The idea is to keep them on the right track of learning which has unfortunately been disrupted by Covid-19.
