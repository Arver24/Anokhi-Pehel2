What is our way of teaching students, what curriculum do we follow?

During the lockdown, we used online mode of Teaching.

We follow the standard NCERT curriculum while teaching and refer other books too in order to improvise the standard of teaching.

In what ways I can contribute?

You can contribute in any way you feel like for example you can donate your old books , some stationary or some helpful guides.

Donors can donate some amount and can know how we can spend it in the wellfare of childrens studying here at Anokhi Pehel.

There are so many NGO's. Why should I support Anokhi पहल?

Anokhi Pehel is a non-profitable organization started by students of MNNIT-Allahabad for the wellfare of studentswho can't afford expensive schools and coachings.

What happens with my donation?

We use your donations to organise events which are helpful for the personality growth of the student and also provide a better way of teaching to them.

Also people here make sure that your donations does not go in wrong hands and your donations really help us to grow as a responsible society which work in favour of helping these childrens.

What is the procedure if I donate from overseas?

You can contact us at our email id given below for overseas donations.

Someone from our teams will attend you as soon sa possible.

What happens with the personal data I enter to make the donation?

We don’t share any data with 3rd party. However your donation can be featured on our website only after your consent.
